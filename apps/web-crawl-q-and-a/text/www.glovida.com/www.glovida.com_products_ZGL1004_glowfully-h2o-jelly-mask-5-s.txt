HOW TO USE:
1) Use on cleansed face.  
2) Open the sachet, take out the mask and carefully unfold.  
3) Remove and discard the protective outer layers.  
4) Gently smooth the mask over the entire face, eliminating any folds or air pockets.  
5) Leave on for 15-20 minutes before removing the mask.  
5) Gently pat any remaining serum into the skin. 
6) No rinsing necessary.
