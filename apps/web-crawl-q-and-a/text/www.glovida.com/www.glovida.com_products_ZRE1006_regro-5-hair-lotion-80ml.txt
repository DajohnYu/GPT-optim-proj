             ADD TO CART  Product Details Description  ReGro is a clear colorless to pale yellow solution which contains minoxidil which when applied topically has been shown to stimulate hair growth in individuals with hair loss for male and female. Observations suggest that the rate of balding progression may also be slowed by the use of this product.

ReGro has developed high-quality formulas to battle concerns such as struggling hair growth. Clinically proven to help regrow hair, this solution will last approximately 40 days upon using.  
Made in Indonesia  
DIRECTIONS:
Adult (18 to 65 years): Apply one dose of 7 sprays (1ml) of ReGro two times a day, preferably morning and night, to the areas of baldness or hair loss. This dose should be used regardless of the size of the bald area. Do not exceed two doses (14sprays or 2ml) per day. Massage scalp gently with fingertips after each spray. Wash hands well after use.  
The method for application as follow:
1. For large area of the scalp, use the pump sprayer. Aim and press the pump towards the center of the balding area. Spread gently with fingertips to cover all the balding areas.
2. For small areas or areas under hair, use the extended tip applicator. Pull the pump sprayer head out and fit the extended tip applicator firmly onto the spray shaft. Aim and press the pump towards the center of the balding area. Spread gently with fingertips to cover all the balding areas.
ReGro should be applied to completely dry hair and scalp. Used only on healthy and intact skin. Do not wash hair or wet the scalp at least 4 hours after application.
Onset and degree of hair growth stimulation vary among individuals.
