Notify Me Product Details Description  A powerful lightweight serum infused with Niacinamide, Snow Fungus and Velvet Horn to provide immediate, deep and lasting hydration for all skin types. This potent formula hydrates, visibly strengthens, firms and improves skin’s defense against irritation and damage.
Oil-free, Fragrance-free, silicone-free
KEY INGREDIENTS:
• ANTARCTICINE: Antarcticine is a rare ingredient derived from glaciers in Antarctica. It is known for its anti-ageing and collagen regeneration properties. Antarcticine provides the appearance of youthful skin by smoothing fine lines and wrinkles. Skin is instantly moisturized and fine lines are diminished.

• SNOW FUNGUS: Snow fungus have similar hydration properties as Hyaluronic acid with its molecules being smaller than that of Hyaluronic acid. This allows the magic ingredient to penetrate deeper into the skin. Snow fungus also inhibit melanin production which fosters to brighten up the skin.

• VELVET HORN: Velvet horn is also known as Codium Tomentosum which is a type of Marine Algae. It instantly provides deep levels of moisture for a prolonged period on the skin. Not only does it provide intensive hydration on the skin, it also plumps up the skin for a healthier glowing skin effect.
• NIACINAMIDE & VITAMIN B5: Niacinamide inhibits the transfer of melanin to the skin surface, preventing the formation of sunspots and freckles. Niacinamide also reduces the appearance of pores, balances oil production and strengthens the skin barrier. Vitamin B5 eliminates the sticky residue left by conventional serums, leaving skin skin soft and moisturised. 
Made in Taiwan
HOW TO USE:
After cleansing and toning, dispense 1-2 drops of Deep Sea Glow Serum and gently pat onto slightly damp face and neck until fully absorbed. Follow with moisturizer to seal everything in place. Apply daily, AM and PM. Keep in a cool, dry place--away from direct light.
