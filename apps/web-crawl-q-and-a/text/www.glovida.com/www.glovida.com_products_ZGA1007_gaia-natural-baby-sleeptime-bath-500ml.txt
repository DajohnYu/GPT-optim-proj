GAIA Natural Baby Sleeptime Bath Wash is designed to do exactly that. It’s made with organic lavender and ylang-ylang which creates a calming bath experience whilst also cleansing their skin, no matter how sensitive or dry. It also contains organic evening primrose oil to make sure baby’s skin stays soft. This gentle cleanser is pH balanced and free from artificial fragrances, soap, sulphates, paraben preservatives, propylene glycol and petrochemicals, making it more suitable for sensitive skin and eczema. Ideal for everyday use.

• Enriched with organic Lavender & Ylang Ylang - an essential oil blend to help encourage relaxation as part of baby's bedtime routine.

• Enriched with organic Chamomile - to help calm and soothe delicate skin.
• The Sleeptime Bath Wash is gentle enough to be used from birth.
• No petrochemicals or artificial fragrance agents making it eczema friendly.
• Cruelty-Free
Made in Australia
DIRECTIONS:
Pour sleeptime bath wash into the running water of the nighttime bath to release the soothing aromas. 
Or add a small amount of sleeptime bath wash to a sponge, washer, or cotton pad and wash your child's skin.
Use in the evening to encourage a regular sleep routine.
