BENEFITS:
• Is traditionally used in aromatherapy to relieve nervous tension and mild anxiety
• Promotes restful sleep and improved sleep quality
• Provides temporarily relief to headaches
• Helps to relieve symptoms of eczema
• Provides relief to skin itching and inflammation of tick, wasp and insect bites
Made in Australia
HOW TO USE:
Adults Only: To help relieve nervous tension, insomnia and give temporary relief of headaches
Massage:  Add 5 drops (1/4mL) to 10mL vegetable oil and massage into temples, forehead, back of neck, shoulders or full body.
Vaporisation: Add 5 drops (1/4mL) to water in an oil burner. 
Bath: Add 5 drops (1/4mL) to bath water.
For the relief of symptoms of eczema: Dilute 5 drops (1/4mL) to 10mL carrier oil.
For relief from tick, wasp and insect bites: Dab undiluted onto affected area.
