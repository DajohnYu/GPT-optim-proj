             ADD TO CART  Product Details Description  Iberet Folic 500 is the complete iron supplementation. A  combination of iron, vitamin C and B complex including Folic Acid. Iberet Folic 500 is a multivitamin indicated for the prevention and treatment of iron deficiency due to poor diet, certain illnesses, or during pregnancy. Formulated as a controlled release product, iron is slowly released from the tablet to faciliate absorption. In addition, Iberet Folic 500 contains Vitamin C which further improves gut iron absorption and folic acid which is essential during pregnancy.
Iberet Folic 500 can be used for treatment of iron deficiency and prevention of concomitant folic acid deficiency in non-pregnant adults. In pregnancy, it can also be used for the prevention and treatment of iron deficiency.
The Complete Iron Supplementation with iron, vitamin C, vitamin B complex and folic acid:
•  Ferrous Sulphat (High elemental iron content) for replenishment of iron stores 
• Vitamin C to enhance iron absorption and for immunity 
•  Vitamin B Complex to help your body to utilize energy through metabolism of carbohydrate, protein & fat
•  Folic Acid to help reduce risk of neural tube defects and to help produce normal red blood cells & prevent anemia
• With controlled release Gradumet technology to improve iron absorption and minimize gastric irritation
